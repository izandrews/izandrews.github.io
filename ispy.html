I-SPY is an interactive artwork that transforms an exhibition into a live surveillance environment. By capturing and transcribing visitor conversations, it positions participants as both observer and observed. The piece makes everyday monitoring tangible, prompting reflection on privacy, visibility, and how invasive surveillance feels when embodied through human presence.
