Q.O.A. is an ongoing photographic and oral archive that centres the queer community through the objects they hold onto. Rather than following institutional hierarchies of value, the project foregrounds everyday, sentimental items. These often overlooked objects not only carry personal memory but also important social and political contexts.

Find the project here: q.o.a.
